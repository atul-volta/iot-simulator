About

IoT SimBox is a browser-based, real-time simulator for generating, visualising and exporting realistic water meter data.
SimBox makes it easy to:

  • Simulate multiple digital water meters with real-world anomaly logic.
  • Inject faults such as low flow, burst, reverse flow and outages to test system reactions.
  • Export readings as CSV/JSON, or enable MQTT for live streaming to your platform.
  • All readings update in real-time.
Open to learners. Open to doers.
Add, simulate and inject faults in digital meters. Export or stream as needed.
