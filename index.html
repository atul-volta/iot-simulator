About IoT SimBox — Water Meter Edition

IoT SimBox is a browser-based, real-time simulator for generating, visualising and exporting realistic water meter data.
Designed for IoT professionals, educators and solution developers, SimBox makes it easy to:

  • Simulate multiple meters with customisable usage patterns
  • Inject faults and status changes for realistic demo scenarios
  • Export data to MQTT brokers (HiveMQ, Mosquitto, and more)
  • Visualise and export readings instantly for analysis and reporting
  • Integrate with Node.js, Python, and major cloud platforms
